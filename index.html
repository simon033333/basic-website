Welcome to
Barangay San Agustin

Barangay San Agustin is a vibrant community located in Alaminos, Laguna. Our commitment is to provide quality services and support to all residents.

Services Offered

Community Programs

We offer various community programs aimed at improving the lives of residents, including workshops, training, and recreational activities.

Health Services

Access to essential health services, including immunizations, health check-ups, and wellness programs.

Public Safety

Our barangay is committed to ensuring the safety of all residents through regular patrols and emergency response services.

Administrative Services

Assistance with documents, permits, and other administrative needs to facilitate smooth community interactions.
